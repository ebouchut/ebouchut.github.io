Baked With Octopress

Feb 10th, 2012

I finally decided to make the move to Octopress.

A lot has been said about this new blogging framework, since last year when I first read about it on Hacker News. To know more about the reasons why I decided to switch my blog from Wordpress to Octopress, you can read the posts by Aijaz Ansari, Matt Gemmell and Mark Nichols.

I intend to use my blog to share my thoughts on all things related to programming. I will also keep you apprised of what I have learned so far about tech and share with you blog posts and podcasts I find interesting.
